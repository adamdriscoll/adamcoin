Get Started

Buying Adams can be done using Tinyman. You need some Algorand (ALGO) in order to make the trade for Adams. Below you’ll find the steps necessary to trade Adams.

First, you’ll need an ALGO wallet. You can download the Pera Wallet from the Android or iOS app store. You can follow the step by step tutorial on how to setup a wallet here.

Once you have a wallet, you’ll need to purchase some ALGO. You can do so either by using a service like Moonpay or by purchasing ALGO in Coinbase. Using Moonpay is nice because it adds directly to your Pera Wallet. If you use Coinbase, you’ll need to send your ALGO from your Coinbase account to your Pera Wallet.

Next, head over to TinyMan and click Connect a Wallet. Select Pera Wallet and then scan the QR code from the Pera Wallet app on your phone.

Once you’ve paired your wallet to TinyMan, you can click the Swap button to trade ALGO for Adams. Click the select a token textbox and then click See all.

Next, click Hide Unverified Assets and search for AdamCoin.

From there, you’ll be able to swap your ALGO for Adams!

You are now the proud owner of Adams.
